# SloerStudio Brand Guidelines

## Company Identity

**SloerStudio** is the parent company and platform brand.
Products are named **Sloer[ProductName]** — always one word, PascalCase.

## Product Names (Exact Spelling)

| Product | Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Desktop IDE | SloerSpace Dev | SloerSpace-Dev, Sloer Space, sloerspace |
| Web Platform | SloerStudio Web | Sloer Studio, SloerStudio-Web |
| Voice App | SloerVoice | Siulk Voice, siulkvoice, SloerVoice™ |
| Swarm Feature | SloerSwarm | Sloer Swarm |
| Canvas Feature | SloerCanvas | Sloer Canvas |

## Logo

File: `brand/logos/company-logo.jpg`

The logo is a **silver triangle-within-triangle mark** (interlocking geometric symbol) on a dark matte background.

- Minimum size: 24×24px
- Clear space: equal to the logo height on all sides
- Do NOT distort, rotate, or recolor the mark
- On dark backgrounds: use the silver version
- On light backgrounds: use the dark version (future)

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct** — No filler phrases. Get to the point.
- **Technical** — Respect developer intelligence. Don't over-explain.
- **Confident** — State things as facts, not suggestions.
- **Human** — We're builders talking to builders.

### ✅ Do
- "Deploy a fleet of AI agents in 5 steps."
- "SloerVoice runs 100% on your device."
- "Ship at the speed of thought."

### ❌ Don't
- "SloerStudio might help you possibly maybe improve your workflow."
- "Exciting new AI-powered features!"
- "Simply click the button to get started!"

## UI Design Principles

1. **Dark first** — Dark mode is the default. Light themes are optional.
2. **No muddy transparencies** — Use solid layered surfaces, not glass blur effects as a primary pattern.
3. **Rounded but grounded** — 12-24px corner radii. Not bubbly.
4. **Information dense** — Maximize content density. Developers want data, not whitespace.
5. **Cobalt as the north star** — The cobalt `#4f8cff` color is always the primary action color.
